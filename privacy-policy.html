CocoaLana - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

CocoaLana ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AutoPublish application. This application is used to schedule and publish product images to social media platforms for our e-commerce business.

Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, TikTok, X) to AutoPublish, we collect:

  • Account usernames and display names
  • OAuth access tokens (stored locally on your device)
  • Published content metadata (post IDs, timestamps, engagement metrics)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Publish content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Display engagement analytics for your published content
  • Manage your publishing schedule

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using a SQLite database. No personal data is collected, stored, or shared with third parties. Your access tokens are stored locally and are only used to communicate directly with the respective platform APIs.

Third-Party Services

AutoPublish interacts with the following third-party APIs on your behalf:

  • Meta Graph API (Instagram, Facebook)
  • Pinterest API
  • X (Twitter) API
  • TikTok API

Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs how they handle your data. This application complies with Pinterest Developer Terms and all applicable platform policies.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account data at any time by removing the account from AutoPublish. This will delete all locally stored tokens and associated data.
